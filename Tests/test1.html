Usability Test Plan Section

Test Plan for: Sinaesthetic

Persona Type Tested: Primary

Context: Persona's own office
Duration: 20 minutes

User has been informed about the product and has signed a consent regarding this test. Background information can be viewed here.

Overall Objectives

Goals of this usability test:

  • Assess the overall ease of use of the product;
  • Identify obstacles in using the tools provided by the product;
  • Assess if the product can be used for a real life task.
Scenario

Persona will try to draw his own company's logo using our product.

Tasks

The task is:

  • Recreate your own company's logo using the tools provided by our product.;
Post-test Questions
  • On a scale of 1 to 5, how did you find our software compared to other software you use normally for this kind of task, by ease of use? (1 means the most difficult to use, 5 means the easiest to use)
  • What is your opinion regarding this statement: "The menu items were well organized and functions were easy to find."?
  • Did you encounter any difficulties in completing the given task? If yes, please elaborate.
  • What are the must-have tools an app such as Sinaesthetic must provide in order to complete the given task?
  • Would you use this app to complete some of your own tasks? Please give details regarding the task and how you might use our product.
Other Notes

Notes taken during the test:

  • - User did not seem to have any trouble in using the app;
  • - User completed the task in half the estimated time;
  • - During the test, user manifested his frustration in regards to the app being too simplistic (the phrase "just a toy" was used);
  • - The end-result was a logo similar to the original, but with much less detail;
  • - User believes the tool is useful for "picturing an idea", creating drafts quickly and then using a more complex tool to create the actual end-product.
