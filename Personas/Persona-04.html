Main Points

Persona is willing to try any new interesting software application and has plenty of time to do so.

Persona has medium educational level, she is a tech-enthusiast and doesn't miss any opportunity to show off with her gadgets.

Persona is pretty popular in her high-school and by discovering this application she has the potential of becoming a trend setter because whenever she meets with her friends one of the points of discussion is about new applications and gadgets.

Goals
  • Practical Goals: procrastinating in an entertaining way
  • Personal Goals: discovering new interesting gadgets and applications
  • Business Goals: -
Detailed Description

Persona is very sociable, she is interested in fashion and one of her interests consists of the new trends and tendencies on every level (clothes, technology etc.). She has a very active social life, as well as the habit to go out very often, she changes her group of friends frequently. Persona is not necessarily interested in the academic life, unless there are other perks, she has a high self esteem and manages to influence those around her.

Frustrations and Pain Points

Some of the difficulties the user might have with the product.

  • The UI might not be sophisticated enough to please her;
  • She can get soaked in and play with it over and over;
  • She will never be a faithful customer, she has to be kept interested.
Scenarios

Persona has come back from school and she remembers some guy recommended her the application. After trying it, she becomes quite interested and uses it for a few days, while telling her friends to try it as well. After the application becomes popular, persona keeps using it because she finds it quite entertaining.

Other Details

Persona's decisions are made according to the latest trends and whenever she finds something worth trying, she manages to be a good leader of word-of-mouth.
