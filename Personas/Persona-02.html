Name: John Smith

Background
  • Date of Birth: 11/21/1977

  • School: Vancouver Island University

Main Points

Deals with a high number of software applications on a daily basis.

Doesn't trust any type of application and the service provider has to be popular.

The working environment is highly stressful and persona needs a source of relaxation in a short period of time.

Goals
  • Practical Goals: increasing the productivity of the company, increasing employees' performance
  • Personal Goals: dealing with deadlines, increasing his own level of productivity, coping with stress
  • Business Goals: coordinating the managerial department as efficiently as possible
Detailed Description

Persona has to work a lot under stress and is aware on how this can affect employees' performance and the business goals and is willing to implement any strategy to help the employees relax in a casual manner. Persona has little or no time for social life, he is exhausted most of the time and hard-working, goal-centered, professional life plays a more important role than personal life, dealing with stress on a daily basis makes him more prone to try new ways of relaxing while at work, the breaks he can afford during the day are not longer than five to ten minutes per hour.

Frustrations and Pain Points

Some of the difficulties the user might have with the product:

  • Having to deal with a large number of applications and software facilities makes persona reluctant to any new products;
  • The lack of popularity of the newly launched product makes getting to persona harder;
  • The product has to be tested in order for the persona to implement it on a larger scale.
Scenarios

After being overwhelmed in the daily board meeting, persona comes back to office and is very stressed. This is when he remembers that someone recommended him this application. While trying it out, he becomes more relaxed and regains the mental disposition required to get back to work. It is then when persona decides to implement the application in the entire company, hoping that anyone would be as pleased as he was.

Other Details

Persona is highly competitive and workaholic, needs competition in order to evolve professionally and cannot afford spending to much time for leisure activities.
